tui.parts PRODUCT DATA SHEET
The primitive and recipe ecosystem for OpenTUI. DOC. NO. TP-001

1. GENERAL DESCRIPTION

tui.parts ships in two halves. Primitives are npm packages that own the difficult, unstyled behavior: state, focus, keyboard and pointer handling, overlays, collections. Every Primitive exposes independently composable public Parts.

Recipes are styled component source you copy into your repo and own outright. Recipes compose Parts and own every glyph, color, and layout decision. Editing the copied source is not a workaround — it is the intended maintenance model.
